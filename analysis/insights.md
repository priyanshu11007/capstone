# 📊 Sales & Weather Analysis – Insights

---

## 1. Impact of Temperature on Sales

- Cold weather shows the highest average sales (~5990), followed by moderate (~5783) and hot (~4533).
- This indicates that **lower temperatures drive higher purchasing activity**.
- Businesses can target promotions during colder periods to maximize revenue.

---

## 2. Monthly Sales Trends

- Sales increase significantly from September to November, peaking in **November (~1.16M)**.
- The lowest sales occur in February (~447K).
- This shows strong **seasonality**, likely due to holiday shopping periods.
- Businesses should focus inventory and marketing strategies during Q4.

---

## 3. City-wise Sales Performance

- **Manchester** is the top-performing city (~8072 avg sales), significantly higher than others.
- Birmingham has the lowest performance (~4950 avg sales).
- This suggests **regional differences in demand**, requiring location-specific strategies.

---

## 4. Impact of Rain on Sales

- Sales during rainy days (~6081) are higher than non-rainy days (~5314).
- This suggests that **bad weather increases shopping activity**, possibly due to more indoor/online purchases.
- Businesses can leverage rainy conditions for targeted promotions.

---

## 5. Top Sales Days (Peak Events)

- The highest sales recorded were:
  - 173K (Manchester, Dec 2023)
  - 81K (Manchester, Jan 2023)
- Manchester dominates peak sales days.
- These spikes may correspond to **special events, promotions, or seasonal demand surges**.

---

## 6. Weekly Trends

- Sales fluctuate across weeks, with peaks around:
  - Week 19 (~6267)
  - Week 20 (~6057)
- Some weeks show significantly lower performance (~2800–3600).
- This indicates **periodic demand variation**, useful for short-term planning.

---

## 7. Best Performing Day of Week

- **Friday (~7459)** is the best-performing day.
- Monday (~3182) has the lowest sales.
- Sunday is completely missing, indicating:
  - Either **no business activity**, or
  - **missing transaction data**
- This highlights **weekend-driven sales behavior**.

---

## 8. Peak Sales Conditions (Combined Factors)

- Highest sales occur when:
  - City: Manchester
  - Weather: Cold or Moderate
  - Rain: Yes
- Example: Manchester + Cold + Rain → ~10239 avg sales
- This shows that **combined factors (location + weather)** strongly influence sales.
- Businesses can use this for **targeted campaigns and forecasting**.

---

## 9. Sales Growth Over Time

- Sales steadily grow throughout 2023, peaking in:
  - November (~1.16M)
- December 2023 shows a drop (~518K), likely due to partial data.
- Indicates:
  - **Strong upward trend**
  - **Seasonal peaks during year-end**

---

# 🔍 Overall Conclusion

- Weather significantly impacts sales, especially temperature and rainfall.
- Manchester is the strongest market across all conditions.
- Sales are highly seasonal, peaking in Q4.
- Fridays and weekends drive the highest revenue.
- Combining weather and location insights enables **better demand forecasting and targeted business strategies**.
